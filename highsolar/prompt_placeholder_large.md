## Style: Large Placeholder Areas (65% right card)

### Prompt:
```
A detailed vector illustration style corporate webpage mockup for solar energy service. Left side (35%) shows friendly smiling male solar technician of South Asian descent, white hard hat, blue work shirt, bright yellow safety vest, holding black tablet. Right side (65%) features a LARGE white rounded information card with LARGE PLACEHOLDER AREAS for text: Top header placeholder bar (bold), main title placeholder bar, then multiple LONGER placeholder bars for bullet points (each bullet has icon placeholder + long text line placeholder), bottom teal banner with placeholder icons. The placeholders should be big and elongated to hold lots of text content later. Background shows house with solar panels on roof, blue sky, sun, clouds, trees. Sunny bright lighting. Clean modern flat design. NO ACTUAL TEXT - only placeholder bars and icon shapes. Large detailed placeholder areas for lengthy content.
```

### Usage:
- Generate image with this prompt to get large placeholder areas in the card
- Text will be added via Facebook post text (NOT in image)
- Good for detailed/long content posts
- 16:9 aspect ratio, 1920x1080 size

### Characteristics:
- Left side (35%): Solar technician character
- Right side (65%): Large white card with big placeholder areas
- No actual text in image - placeholders only
- Background: House with solar panels, blue sky, sun, clouds, trees
- Clean, modern, flat design style
