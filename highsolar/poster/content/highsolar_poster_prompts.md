```
A clean and modern flat-lay style infographic. The image features a bright sun on a light blue sky with stylized white clouds. Below, a modern white house with a terracotta tiled roof is depicted with a detailed array of blue-grey solar panels. Next to it, a smaller matching outbuilding is shown. The foreground is a well-kept green lawn with small bushes and a few stylized ornamental trees. In the background, there is a cluster of colorful, whimsical tree or leaf shapes. ## Prompt เปรียบเทียบ (มี vs ไม่มี Solar)
```

```
A clean and modern flat-lay style infographic contrasting a house without solar panels on the left and the same house with solar panels on the right. Left side: a white house with terracotta roof but no solar panels, with a large red electricity bill shown, red upward arrow indicating rising costs, and a frowning sun hidden behind grey clouds. Right side: same house with beautiful blue-grey solar panels on the roof, bright yellow sun with rays, green downward arrows indicating savings, green plants and eco icons. The comparison is clear, text-free, vectorized flat design, eco-friendly aesthetic, bright and cheerful on the solar side, dull and concerning on the no-solar side.
```

**ใช้กับ:** โพสเปรียบเทียบ มี/ไม่มีโซลาร์เซลล์
